From the moment I stepped on a skateboard, I felt an unexplainable rush, a freedom that transcended words. It wasn’t just about the sport; it was a culture, a lifestyle that embraced individuality and creativity. That initial push on the board became the catalyst for a lifelong journey.

Growing up, skateboarding wasn’t just a hobby; it became my solace, my sanctuary amidst life’s chaos. It provided a canvas for expression, a way to navigate the world and discover myself. Through scraped knees and triumphant tricks, it taught me resilience, perseverance, and the beauty of embracing failure as a stepping stone to success.

As I traversed my own personal skateboarding odyssey, I couldn’t shake off the profound impact it had on shaping my character. It wasn’t merely about the flips or the grinds; it was about the community, the unspoken bond among skateboarders worldwide. We were a tribe, united by a passion for defying norms and pushing boundaries.

In the midst of this profound journey, a seed of purpose began to sprout within me. I witnessed how skateboarding could transform lives, especially for the youth. It wasn’t just a sport; it was a haven, a means to channel energy positively. The camaraderie and inclusivity inherent in skate culture made it a powerful tool for empowerment.

That seed blossomed into a vision—a shop, not merely a storefront, but a haven. A place where skateboards weren’t just commodities but instruments of change. It’s not solely about profit margins; it’s about nurturing dreams, fostering a community, and providing a space for self-expression and growth.

Opening this shop isn’t just a business venture; it’s a mission, a heartfelt commitment to give back to a culture that shaped me. It’s about making skateboarding accessible to every young enthusiast, ensuring they have the tools and support to chase their passion. Whether it’s providing affordable gear, organizing community events, or offering mentorship programs, it’s about creating opportunities where none existed before.

My autobiography intertwines with the hum of spinning wheels and the clatter of skateboards hitting concrete. It’s a story of personal transformation, resilience, and the profound impact of a simple piece of wood with wheels. And through this shop, I aim to script a chapter that echoes beyond commerce, resonating with the laughter and aspirations of every young skateboarder who walks through the door.

The skateboard shop aims to foster community engagement by providing affordable, quality gear and hosting events that promote inclusivity. Our goal is to create a safe space where youth can explore their passion for skateboarding, offering mentorship programs and workshops to cultivate skills beyond the sport.

We aspire to support local talent, sponsoring events and collaborating with artists to showcase their work.

Through partnerships with schools and youth organizations, we aim to encourage physical activity and creativity, using skateboarding as a tool for personal development. Ultimately, our business aims to empower and uplift the community, nurturing a vibrant, supportive skate culture.

Meet The Team

Razien MacKenzie, the CEO of SK-Infinitely, aspires to sell skateboards not just as a business venture but as a beacon of hope for his community in Manenberg. Growing up in Manenberg has instilled in him a profound understanding of the challenges faced by the youth in underserved areas. Skateboarding was his escape, a transformative force that reshaped his life.

He envisions SK-Infinitely not merely as a skateboard shop, but as a catalyst for change. Having experienced firsthand the positive impact skateboarding can have on shaping one's character and providing an outlet for creativity, Razien is driven by the desire to share this transformative experience with the youth of Manenberg and beyond.

SK-Infinitely's mission goes beyond commerce; it's about empowering the community, offering opportunities for the youth to explore their passion for skateboarding while providing mentorship, support, and a safe space for self-expression. Razien believes that by nurturing a vibrant skateboarding culture, SK-Infinitely can contribute to breaking barriers, fostering inclusivity, and uplifting the spirits of the community he holds dear. Through this endeavor, Razien aims to inspire and pave the way for a brighter future for the youth of Manenberg and similar communities, showing them that their potential is infinite.
